# RC 문항 원장

이 문서는 RC 문항 단위 기록을 누적하는 append-only 원장이다.

| item_id | attempt_id | session_id | date | source_anchor | part | question_no | question_type | skill_tag | vocab_domain | document_genre | result | error_type | time_pressure | qna_id | concept_card | review_state |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

## 필드 규칙
- `result`: `correct / wrong / uncertain`
- `error_type`: `grammar / vocabulary / paraphrase / connect-info / inference / evidence-location / time-pressure / mixed`
- `review_state`: `new / reviewed / repeated / stabilized`
- `document_genre`는 Part 7이 아니면 `n/a`를 쓴다.
